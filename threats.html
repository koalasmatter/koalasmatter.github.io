Threats

Loss of Habitat (Deforestation)

Australia has lost 45% of it's forests in 200 years - there needs to be stronger laws in place (mainly in NSW and QLD) to stop koalas losing their habitat due to deforestation!

Dogs

Koalas get very stressed when dogs are around. Koalas get hurt/killed by dogs. One way koalas may get hurt is when people leave their dogs in their yard and koalas come in and get hurt by them.

Cars

When Koalas lose their habitat they're very likely to be hit by cars when trying to find a new habitat.

Did you know?

Did you know that there are laws in QLD where pool owners must meet certain requirments if the pool is in a certain area due to Koalas!
